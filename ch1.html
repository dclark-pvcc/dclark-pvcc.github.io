Three misconceptions about Computer Science:
  1. Computer Science is the study of computers
  2. Computer science is the study of how to write computer programs
  3. Computer science is the study of the uses and applications of computers and software
Briefly define:
  1. Studying algorithms in order to indentify and solve important problems aswell as design and build computer systems
  2. A set of effectively ordered operations that start and halt at specific times
  3. Instructions are carried out in a direct way, going from step 1 to 2 then 3
  4. Intructions check if a factor is met and decides whether or not to proceed
  5. Intructions set will repeat previous instructions
  6. Represented as binary string which is stored in the computer's memory
  7. The occupation of someone who writes commands in a high-level language
  8. Transistors, resistors, and capacitors all interconnected on a single piece of semiconductor material
Briefly describe these computer generations:
  1. 1950-1957: First commercial use computers
  2. 1957-1965: Size reduction, first operating systems
  3. 1965-1975: Integrated circuits, first minicomputers, apperance of software industry
  4. 1975-1985: Reduction in size and cost, computer networks, growth in hardware and software
  5. 1985-present: Supercomputers, worlwide networks, widespread use of digitized sound, images, and movies
